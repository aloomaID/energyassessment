Madera Ranchos, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Madera Ranchos is a unincorporated community in Madera County, California, United States. It lies at an elevation of 341 feet (104 m). As of the 2020 census it had a population of 3,623. For statistical purposes, the United States Census Bureau has defined Madera Ranchos as a census-designated place (CDP). Prior to 2020, Madera Ranchos was part of the Bonadelle Ranchos-Madera Ranchos CDP.
