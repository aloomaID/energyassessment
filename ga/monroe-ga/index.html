How to pick the best solar installer

First, research the installer’s experience and reputation. Look for companies that have been in business for several years and have positive reviews from satisfied customers. This background will give you confidence in their ability to deliver quality work.
Next, check for proper certifications and licenses. A reliable solar installer should have certifications from recognized organizations, such as the North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners (NABCEP). This ensures they are knowledgeable about industry standards and best practices.
It's also crucial to compare quotes from multiple installers in Monroe Georgia . While cost should not be the only factor, obtaining several estimates can help you understand the market rate and the specific services included in each offer. Ensure that the proposals detail the equipment used, installation timeline, and warranties offered.
Additionally, inquire about the installer's warranty and maintenance services. A reputable installer should provide robust warranties on both the panels and the installation work, along with options for ongoing maintenance.
Lastly, look for an installer that offers customized solutions tailored to your energy needs.
